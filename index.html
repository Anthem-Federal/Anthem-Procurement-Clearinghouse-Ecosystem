THE CLEARINGHOUSE MODEL
Anthem Federal is the prime contractor and financial clearinghouse at the center of the ecosystem. When a Contracting Officer approves a purchase on Warfighter Prime, Anthem Federal fronts the payment immediately — before the government disbursement cycle completes. Anthem is made whole when the government pays. The vendor gets paid in hours, not months. This is the innovation.
WHY THIS IS THE ARMY'S MODEL
The Army's RFI asked for a commercially viable, self-sustaining operating model where the Army acts as an anchor customer without appropriated funds being the sole ROI. Anthem Federal generates commercial marketplace revenue, platform licensing, and data services revenue — diversifying away from appropriations dependency while the Army remains a stable, long-term partner.

CLAUSAL — FAR/DFARS COMPLIANCE ANALYZER
Every clause. Every regulation. Every transaction. Verified at machine speed.

CLAUSAL is the FAR/DFARS compliance analysis engine powering Warfighter Prime's checkout and ORCHARD's validation phase. It cross-references every acquisition package against current regulations, identifies missing clauses, flags conflicts, and generates a compliance report — automatically, before any human reviewer touches the file.

OPEN LIVE DEMO →
WHAT CLAUSAL ANALYZES
FAR Parts 1–53, DFARS Parts 201–253, agency supplements, clause matrix requirements, deviation tracking, and cross-reference validation against the most current published regulations.
HOW IT INTEGRATES
CLAUSAL runs automatically when a Warfighter Prime cart is submitted and again during ORCHARD's validation phase. Results are logged immutably as part of the transaction audit trail.
WHY IT MATTERS
Manual FAR/DFARS compliance review is the single biggest bottleneck in DoD acquisition. CLAUSAL eliminates the bottleneck — turning hours of legal review into seconds of automated verification.
THE AUDIT CONNECTION
Every CLAUSAL compliance result becomes a permanent part of the acquisition record, satisfying GAO and IG audit requirements for documented compliance review on every transaction.

HOW FEDBIT WORKS
Congressional Approval
Congress approves the DoD budget. The Federal Mint is triggered to create the corresponding Fedbit allocation instantly — no manual process.
Instant Agency Dispersal
Fedbit disperses to agencies upon budget passage, allocated like a credit card. No manual allotment delays.
CO Approval Trigger
When the Contracting Officer signs, Fedbit releases from agency wallet to Anthem Federal clearinghouse immediately.
Vendor Wallet Credit
Anthem Federal credits the vendor's Fedbit wallet. New vendors serve a 30-day escrow as part of qualification.
DFAS Auto-Reconciliation
Every Fedbit transaction auto-reconciles with DFAS records at machine speed. Zero unreconciled entries. Zero manual work.
WHAT FEDBIT IS NOT
Fedbit is not a cryptocurrency. No speculative value. No market volatility. No mining. It is a permissioned, government-backed digital ledger token — 1:1 USD, minted only by congressional appropriation, settled exclusively through Federal Reserve infrastructure.
